Hold space to charge a jump, release to jump. Longer charge = stronger jump. 

The number on platforms represent how many times you can jump off of it. If it hits 0, the platforms  is destroyed

If the platform doesn't have a number, it is not mortal and you can jump off of it as many times as you'd like.

The goal is to get to the flag

Hold left mouse button to charge gun and release to shoot. The longer you charge the higher the force of the bullet.

Colored platforms are platforms that you can't get stuck to or jump off and needs to be removed by shooting its key. Its key is identified as a small square of the same color.

Hit R to go into pan mode and scout the level

ESC will bring up the pause menu where you can skip or restart the current level or go back to the main menu.
